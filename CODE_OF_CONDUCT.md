# Tech Assassin: Code of Conduct

## The Creed
We are elite operatives dedicated to dismantling technical monoliths and claiming the digital throne. To maintain our edge, we operate with precision, respect, and mutual support. Every operative, regardless of rank or background, is a vital link in our neural network.

## Operational Standards
All operatives are expected to:
- **Execute with Integrity**: Be respectful of differing opinions and viewpoints.
- **Share the Intel**: Give and gracefully accept constructive, high-fidelity feedback.
- **Own the Deployment**: Accept responsibility for mistakes and learn from every mission.
- **Protect the Squad**: Foster a harassment-free environment for everyone.

## Unacceptable Protocol
The following behaviors are considered a breach of system integrity:
- Harassment, bullying, or personal attacks against fellow operatives.
- Distribution of private intelligence (doxing) without authorization.
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments.
- Any conduct that compromises the professional standards of the collective.

## Mission Enforcement
Breaches of the Creed will be handled by Command. Actions may include:
1. **System Alert**: A private warning regarding behavior.
2. **Neural Disconnect**: Temporary ban from community spaces and communication.
3. **Permanent De-listing**: Permanent removal from the Tech Assassin network.
